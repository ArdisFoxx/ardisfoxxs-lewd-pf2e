In Foundry setup, navigate to the Modules tab.
Click Install Module.
Enter the below link in the Manifest URL at the bottom of the window, then click Install.

https://github.com/ArdisFoxx/ardisfoxxs-lewd-pf2e/releases/download/v1.0.0/module.json

Launch your Foundry world.
Activate the ArdisFoxx's Lewd PF2e module.
Import the compendiums. (If you don't want to import everything, at a minumum, the Cumflation macro only works if the Cumflation effects are imported)
